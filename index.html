PARG School

Date of 1st Openin - 6/17/2004 , 12:00:00 AM

The exuberant wish of Rani Sahiba Devyaniraje Gaekwar to propitiate congenially her fond memory of her scholarly husband,Late Prince Ashokraje Gaekwad the grandson of the Late His Highness Maharaja Sir Sayajirao Gaekwad III,the king of Vadodara ,effloresced into the institution of,Prince Ashokraje Gaekwad School,Vadodara.Situated on the verdant, eco friendly and sprawling campus of Dhairya Prasad Palace,located beside the Lalbaug main road, Vadodara,Prince Ashokraje Gaekwad School,Vadodara effuses conducive educative environment to engender education with ease.

Class Rooms

Class rooms are maintained very nicely

The School avows that Teaching is not telling . The entire school community avers that teaching is a misnomer and facilitating learning is the practical reality. As one more step in this direction ,the school has made all the classrooms into smart classrooms,which involves effective use of technology , to ensure that the classroom transaction is bolstered with exhaustive and effective audio visual aids. This will make the learning process very insightful and thereby enjoyable.
